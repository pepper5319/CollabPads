CollabPads is a new way to share lists with friends and family

pads. Much more than a list

CollabPads doesn't use traditional lists. Pad's give users control over how others can contribute to and access items. Best of all they can be viewed anywhere, anytime, on any device.

share with anyone in any way

Collaborating on something has never been easier. Share with anyone, whether they have an account or not. Signing up gives users more features, but they can contribute to any Pad as long as they are granted access.

In Development

Since CollabPads is still in development, some features still haven't been implemented or are in progress (which means a bug could be crawling around here or there). However, we intend to fully implement each feature in order to deliver a great piece service.
